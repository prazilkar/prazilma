Features of AI Virtual Mouse

  • Real-Time Hand Tracking: Tracks hand landmarks live with high accuracy using Mediapipe.
  • Cursor Control: Move the mouse pointer based on your handâ€™s position.
  • Left Click: Touch your thumb and index finger together to perform a single click.
  • Double Click: Quick consecutive clicks are automatically detected.
  • Right Click: Touch your thumb and middle finger together to perform a right click.
  • Scrolling: Bring thumb and pinky closer to activate vertical scrolling with hand motion.
  • Full-Screen Display: Full immersive interaction window for better usability.
  • Smooth Cursor Movement: Adaptive smoothing for faster, more natural cursor motion.
  • Smart Lost Frame Handling: Continues moving in last known direction if hand temporarily lost.
  • Threaded Frame Capture: Improves camera reading speed with background threading.
  • Customizable Click Cooldowns: Avoid accidental multiple clicks through timing control.
  • High FPS Display: Frame rate monitor for performance debugging.
